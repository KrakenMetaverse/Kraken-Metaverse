INTRODUCTION

Welcome to Kraken Metaverse, a P2E action role-playing game. Players can stake, fight and upgrade their playable characters and weapon by playing the game. The game is powered by solana, beacuse of its known speed of up to 50,000 TPS and transaction fees are relatively low.

Players will aim to collect as many genes as they can to upgrade there characters, upgrading characters will influence the outcome of matches, as a player with a stronger character has more probability to win.

All game Characters and Genes are NFTs and can be traded in the marketplace for real monetary value. A player can choose to upgrade his character and trade it in the marketplace for actual money.
